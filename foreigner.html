THE FORIEGBNER

BIOGRAPHY

Full Name:CHAN KONG-SANG

Place Of Birth:7 April 1954 (age 67 years), Victoria Peak, Hong Kong

Gender: Male

Characters:Froggy LeSueur,Owen Musser,Ellard,Catherine Simms,Betty Meeks,Charlie Baker,Reverend David Lee

Summary:Quan is a humble London businessman whose long-buried past erupts in a revenge-fueled vendetta when the only person left for him to love -- his teenage daughter -- dies in a senseless act of politically motivated terrorism. His relentless search to find the terrorists leads to a cat-and-mouse conflict with a British government official whose own past may hold the clues to the identities of the elusive killers.
